KBC Instructions

  • You must register with your name and a photo (either captured or uploaded) to begin the quiz.
  • The quiz consists of 7 questions, randomly selected from the database.
  • Each correct answer awards you points. The points increase as you answer each question.
  • Incorrect answers will immediately end the game and redirect you to the results page.
  • You can use a clue to eliminate two incorrect options, but it will cost you 5,000 points.
  • Ensure you answer all questions before the timer runs out! If you take too long, you will be redirected to the results page.
  • The goal is to answer all 7 questions correctly to earn the highest possible score!
